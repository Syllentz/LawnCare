Golden Rules

Mowing
  • Cut at 1–2" always
  • Never remove more than 1/3 of the blade
  • Mulch clippings (except spring scalp)
  • Alternate direction every mow
  • Mow mid-morning when dry
Watering
  • 1–1.25" per week total
  • 2–3 deep sessions, not daily
  • Early morning only (5–10 AM)
  • Never water in the evening (fungus)
  • Signs of thirst: blue-gray color, blades folding
Never Do This
  • Fertilize brown/dormant Bermuda
  • Apply nitrogen after August
  • Dethatch or aerate dormant grass
  • Mow wet or frozen grass
  • Skip pre-emergent (crabgrass will win)
Spreader Application Tips
  • Use broadcast (rotary) spreader
  • Two passes at half rate: N-S then E-W
  • Apply on dry grass, water in within 24 hrs
  • Sweep spills off sidewalk/driveway
  • Don't apply before heavy rain
